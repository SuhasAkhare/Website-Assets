We transform your designs into data-rich, intelligent 3D models that streamline coordination, reduce risk, and ensure project-wide clarity from concept to completion.

What is Building Information Modeling?

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is an intelligent 3D model-based process that gives architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) professionals the insight and tools to more efficiently plan, design, construct, and manage buildings and infrastructure. It's more than just a 3D model; it's a digital representation of the physical and functional characteristics of a facility, serving as a shared knowledge resource for reliable decision-making throughout a building's lifecycle.

Our Comprehensive BIM Modeling Services

We provide end-to-end BIM modeling services across all major disciplines, ensuring a single source of truth for your project.

Architectural BIM

From conceptual massing to detailed construction models, we model all architectural elements, including walls, doors, windows, and finishes with precision.

Structural BIM

We create detailed structural models including foundations, framing, rebar, and connections ready for analysis and fabrication.

MEPF BIM

We develop coordinated models for Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, and Firefighting systems for seamless integration and clash avoidance.

Specialized Modeling Capabilities

Scan to BIM

For renovation and retrofit projects, we accurately convert point cloud data from laser scans into intelligent, as-is condition Revit models.

As-Built Modeling

Post-construction, we update the model to reflect final built conditions and changes, creating a valuable digital asset for facility management.

Levels of Development (LOD)

We tailor our models to your project's specific needs, delivering precise Levels of Development (LOD) from conceptual design to facility management:

  • LOD 100: Conceptual Model for massing studies.
  • LOD 200: Schematic Design with approximate quantities, size, and location.
  • LOD 300: Detailed Design with specific assemblies and precise data.
  • LOD 350: Construction Documentation including details for coordination.
  • LOD 400: Fabrication & Assembly with complete fabrication details.
  • LOD 500: As-Built Model verified in the field for maintenance and operations.
